CAGR Calculation (Python)


def cagr(begin, end, years):
    """Compound Annual Growth Rate"""
    return (end / begin) ** (1 / years) - 1

print(cagr(100, 150, 3))

Financial Math — LaTeX

Summation

$$ \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i $$

Standard Deviation

$$ \sigma = \sqrt{ \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^n (x_i - \mu)^2 } $$

Expected Return

$$ E(R) = \sum_{i=1}^n p_i r_i $$

NPV

$$ \mathrm{NPV} = \sum_{t=0}^T \frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t} $$

Forecast Model

Start: \(R_0\)

Growth Rate: \(g\)

Projected: $$ R_n = R_0 (1+g)^n $$
